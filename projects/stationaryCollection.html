My Pen Collection

Item Brand Nib Details
x86 JinHao Stub Got 3, gave 2 away. I ground the one I kept into a stub nib.
10 Red Ice JinHao M Originally planned to sand and refinish brown, but have kept for now.
10 Silver Pinstripe JinHao F A surprise gift from my wife!
Axel S Spoke M I really enjoyed my Spoke Clickstream, and wanted to try their fountain pens. I adored my Kaweco Sport and figured Brad "pocket pen" Dowdy knew what he was doing.
Clickstream Spoke Ohto Flash Dry Another creation by PenAddict. Love the satisfying click; however, I also wish there was a clip.
Vanishing Point Pilot F My first 'grail' pen. Got it in black (of course). My wedding present and still my favorite pen.
Plaisir Platinum F Honestly the first fountain pen I've been let down by. Loved the Preppy (until I lost it in a move), but can’t believe a 'nicer' barrel somehow makes the pen worse.
Metropolitan Pilot M My first fountain pen (as I’m sure it is for many people). Solid, built well, and cheap back then. Now I don’t know what I’d recommend for a first pen.
Sport Kaweco M The first "pocket" pen I acquired. I usually carry my pens clipped to my shirt, so I figured maybe a small, light fountain pen would work. It is fantastic.
Jotter Parker Parker refill My father got me this pen to celebrate my first home purchase. I hate the refill and use anything other than it.
Classique Ballpoint Mont Blanc Mont Blanc M refill An absolute steal on eBay. My most used non-fountain pen. Doesn’t write super well and I prefer Schmidt P8126 refills, but something keeps pulling me back to it.
Tornado Retro 51 Schmidt P8126 The only pen I own two of. One in my work bag in case the pen of the day stops working. Bought a second thinking I lost the first, only to find it later in a suitcase.
Safari Black Lamy EF Going from the heavy Metropolitan to this was a shock, but I preferred the nib and wanted the all-black limited edition of that year.
9019 Silver JinHao F Wanted a hooded nib pen. While the Parker 51 is still on my someday list, I wanted to see if I even liked it. $10 for 2 of these was worth it.
x250 Silver JinHao F My first ever JinHao. Pleasantly surprised. Very suspicious at first, but it’s a great silver and gold pen with heft and writes well.
Mark One Studio Neat Schmidt P8126 Love Studio Neat’s designs and backed the project. Luckily, the first run came in black. It’s fine, but I wish it had a clip. Slight wobble when writing like the nosecone tolerances are off.
x750 Sparkles JinHao F A little Christmas present to myself. I should have returned it—the fit and finish is so poor, I hate this pen and don’t like the sparkles either.
Eco Matcha TWSBI M A special edition, and how could I not make it my TWSBI.
White A1 Majohn Architect Grind Got specifically to grind myself.
